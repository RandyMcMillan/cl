CL - 3D Digital Clock

The beauty is in the simplicity

CL turns your iOS device into a nice-looking 3D clock. It's beautiful, it's simple, and it'll never distract you.

Get CL at App Store for free. It gives you an alternative use for your i-devices as the finest interior goods.

Features

  • 12/24 hour display
  • Night mode
  • Tick sounds
  • Hourly chime
  • Ad-free
